Your Distillery Journey

Discover the heart and soul of Ninefold Distillery, a place where local tradition meets global innovation. This experience is designed to inspire, educate, and immerse you in the world of premium Scottish rum.

During your visit, you’ll learn about our copper-pot stills, sustainable practices, and the craft that goes into every drop we produce. But the experience doesn't stop when you leave:

  • Each bottle purchased includes a plantable label infused with wildflower seeds.
  • Take it home, plant it in soil, and watch your own little patch of nature grow as a symbol of Scotland’s commitment to sustainability.

Ready to raise a glass to the future? Book your spot below and grow the impact of every bottle you enjoy.
